WhatsApp us your measurements and we'll pick the best size for you!

👔 Men's Dress Code Tips

  • 🔵 Casual: Shalwar Kameez, printed T-shirts, jeans
  • ⚫ Smart Casual: Linen shirts, chinos, formal kurta
  • 🟠 Eid/Wedding: Embroidered kurta, waistcoat + shalwar
  • ✅ Tip: If between sizes, go one size up for comfort

📐 How to Measure

📏
Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping tape parallel to floor.

📏
Waist

Measure the narrowest part of your waist, usually just above the navel.

📏
Hips

Measure around the widest part of your hips, about 8 inches below waist.

👗 Women's Dress Code Tips

  • 🌸 Casual: Lawn suit, printed kurta with tights
  • 💜 Semi-Formal: Embroidered suit, chiffon dupatta
  • 🟠 Eid/Wedding: Full embroidered 3-piece, silk dupatta
  • ✅ Tip: Pakistani suits run slightly large — check chest size
